About Me

I am a Master's student in Advanced Computing at Morgan State University, and currently a Research Associate at the Center for Equitable AI and Machine Learning Systems. My research focuses on responsible AI, generative models, and multimodal medical image analysis, with real-world applications in healthcare, disaster management, and additive manufacturing.

Research Interests

  • Generative AI: image synthesis, GANs, defect modeling
  • Multimodal Learning: vision–language models, radiology captioning, VQA
  • Explainable AI: interpretable models for high-stakes domains
  • Computer Vision: medical image classification and analysis
  • Deep Learning: transformers, efficient model fine-tuning
